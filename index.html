Our Story

Rooted in Tradition, Crafted with Devotion

At Jeyam Candle Works Melur, we believe a candle is more than just wax and a wick; it is a messenger of light, carrying prayers and bringing peace to your home.

Born in Melur, we have dedicated ourselves to perfecting the art of candle making. Inspired by the divine structure of Indian temples and rituals, our candles are hand-poured, chemical-less, and ensure a steady, spiritual flame.

  • Pure & Chemical-less

    Safe to burn in enclosed spaces like pooja rooms.

  • Divine Ambiance

    Crafted specifically for prayers, meditation, and festive vibes.

  • Long Lasting Glow

    Designed for extended burn times with minimal smoke.
